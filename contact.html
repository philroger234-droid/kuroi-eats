FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Quick answers to common questions

What areas do you deliver to? +

We currently deliver within a 10-mile radius of our kitchen locations. Enter your address on our homepage to check if we deliver to your area.

How long does delivery take? +

Our average delivery time is 30 minutes. During peak hours, it may take up to 45 minutes. You can track your order in real-time through our website.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions? +

Yes! We offer vegetarian options and can accommodate some allergies. Please mention any dietary restrictions in the order notes.

What is your cancellation policy? +

Orders can be cancelled within 5 minutes of placing them. After that, the kitchen may have already started preparing your food.
